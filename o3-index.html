FAQ

Which fridges or freezers are compatible?
Any glass-door unit with <30 mm frame depth. VisionVend attaches with VHB tape—no drilling.
How long do the batteries last?
24-48 h on Li-po batteries, but we ship with USB-C power supply for continuous use. Super-capacitor keeps the lock closed if mains fails.
Can it work offline?
Yes. Object detection runs locally; if LTE is down, transactions are queued and sync automatically once re-connected.
How do I add new products?
Add a row to the Google Sheet, press “Restock-Train”, close the door. VisionVend captures 120 frames and retrains overnight.
